Hi, I am Evlyne Muchiri,

Eve Muchiri is a transformation life coach specializing in growth and self-awareness from the understanding that healing, success, self-love, peace, and joy begin from self-awareness. Achieving self-awareness means that you understand your strengths, weaknesses, motivations, blind spots, and triggers well enough to shape your thoughts and beliefs in ways that empower you to have a life that is yours, a life that you are proud of.

Eve uses practical, simple, and relatable coaching techniques and tools that will guide you to discover who you are, why you are who you are, why you think and behave as you do, and to empower you come up with the most optimal solutions to challenges and opportunities in various areas of your life.

Eve coaches because she is passionate about improving people's lives in simple, practical, and accessible ways. She has learnt how to get moving and recover well from setbacks through everyday activities such as mustering the discipline to run, transitioning from a successful sales career to life coaching, creating boundaries and saying no, and learning how to ask for help, among other marginal improvements in her personal and work life that have given her hands-on experience to support her work as a Transformation Life Coach.

Eve has a natural aptitude for coaching due to her critical thinking abilities, empathy, enthusiasm, and positivity toward life. Her experience in sales and marketing exposed her to dynamic environments that require quick thinking and a solution-oriented mindset, and numerous interpersonal relationships that have made her non-judgemental, personable, mindful, bold, and open-minded.

Transformative life coaching is Eve, and Eve is transformative life coaching. It is close to her heart.

BIGGEST BARRIER TO CHANGE AND TRANSFORMATION

Comfort zones are tempting to choose because change is not easy. The discomfort in challenging yourself is the biggest barrier because the coaching process is more internal than it is external. Accepting that the transformation process will have some painful moments and not running away from the pain of growth cannot be easy. Remaining truthful with yourself about where you are and what you need to do to get the goals and dreams you desire is difficult. But,

  1. if you are ready to have different results in your life,
  2. if you are ready to show up as the person you know you are and can be if you were not afraid and self-sabotaging,
  3. if you are ready to build your confidence and self-esteem,
  4. if you are ready to have a beautiful and loving relationship with yourself and others,

the transformation journey you are about to begin will be life changing and every challenge along the way worth it.

EVE HELPS YOU...

  1. Develop positive thoughts and set your world on a positive trajectory.
  2. Identify and define practical and achievable goals.
  3. Become a better husband, wife, girlfriend, boyfriend, parent, manager, employee, employer, friend, sibling.
  4. Become resilient and confident.
  5. Understand self-care, self-love, and self-worth.
